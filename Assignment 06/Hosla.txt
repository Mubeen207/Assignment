1. "Aur jab mera banda tumse mere baare mein poochay to (keh do) main qareeb hoon. Main pukarne walay ki dua qabool karta hoon jab wo mujhe pukarta hai..."
📖 (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:186)

2. "Aur jo Allah se daray, Allah uske liye nikalnay ka rasta bana deta hai. Aur use wahan se rizq ata karta hai jahan se uska gumaan bhi na ho."
📖 (Surah At-Talaq 65:2-3)

3. "Aur na umeed na ho, beshak Allah ki rahmat se sirf woh log mayoos hotay hain jo kafir hain."
📖 (Surah Yusuf 12:87)

4. "Beshak har mushkil ke sath asani hai, yaqeenan har mushkil ke sath asani hai."
📖 (Surah Al-Inshirah 94:5-6)

5. "Agar Allah tumhari madad kare to koi tum par ghalib nahi aa sakta, aur agar woh tumhe chhod de to uske baad kaun hai jo tumhari madad kare? Aur momineen sirf Allah hi par bharosa karein."
📖 (Surah Aal-e-Imran 3:160)

6. "Aur tum apne Rab ki ibadat karo jab tak maut tumhare paas na aa jaye."
📖 (Surah Al-Hijr 15:99)

7. "Aur jab tum ghamgeen hote ho, to sirf Allah ki taraf rujoo karo, kyunki wahi tumhara sabse behtareen madadgaar hai."
📖 (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:45)

8. "Kaho! Allah hi humare liye kaafi hai, aur wahi behtareen kaarsaz hai."
📖 (Surah Aal-e-Imran 3:173)

9. "Aur jo Allah par bharosa kare, Allah uske liye kaafi hai."
📖 (Surah At-Talaq 65:3)

"Aur yaqeenan hum tumhe kuch dar (khauf), bhook, maal aur jaanon ki kami, aur phalon ki kami se azmaenge. Aur (aey Nabi ﷺ) aise sabr karne walon ko khushkhabri suna do."
📖 (Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:155)

➜ Matlab: Yeh dukh sirf ek azmaish hai. Agar tum isme sabr rakhoge, to Allah tumhe iska behtareen badla dega.

jo log aaj tumhare sath na-insafi kar rahe hain, Allah unke faislon ka hisaab lega. Tumhare jazbaat bekaar nahi jayenge, Allah tumhare sabr ka sila zaroor dega. 

Allah ka farman hai ke jo log duniya me zulm ya na-insafi karte hain, wo apne amal ka anjaam zaroor dekhenge. Allah har cheez ka hisaab lega, aur kisi ka bhi haq zaya nahi hone dega

"Aur tumhara Rab zalimon se bekhabar nahi hai."
📖 (Surah Hud, 11:11)

"Aur zulm karne walon ka anjaam bohot bura hoga."
📖 (Surah Ibrahim, 14:42)

Allah ne waada kiya hai ke sabr karne walon ko behtareen sila milega:

"Yaqeenan Allah sabr karne walon ke saath hai."
📖 (Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:153)

Aur jo kisi par zarra barabar zulm karega, uska badla use diya jayega."
📖 (Surah Az-Zalzalah, 99:8)
